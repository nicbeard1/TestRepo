Background

Lego ia a toy company from Billund, Denmark.

This world-famous toy company specialises in construction toys for a wide range of popular licensed themes, such as Star Wars, DC and Marvel comics E.T.C., and original themes such as DreamzZz and Ninjago. Founded by a carpenter called Ole Kirk Christiansen in 1932, the company originally created and sold wooden toys, the most famour of which being a colourful wheeled duck on a string. lego introduced their plastic construction toys in 1948, providing a range of vehicles such as cars and trucks. in 1978, lego introduced the minifigure, a small, poseable figure, one or multiple of which were and still are included in sets. Since then, lego has released books, magazines, TV series and movies based on their characters, both original characters such as Clutch Powers and Emmett Brickowski, and licensed themes such as star wars.
